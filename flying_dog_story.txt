Luna the golden retriever never imagined she could fly. One misty morning, shimmering wings appeared on her back. Terrified at first, she soon discovered the pure joy of soaring through fluffy clouds. Below, her quiet neighborhood appeared tiny and magical. She performed joyful loop-de-loops with songbirds, chased butterflies between tall buildings, and painted the sky with her endless enthusiasm. The wind carried her higher than any dog had ever gone. She wasn't just flying; she was dancing with the world itself. When sunset painted everything gold and purple, Luna descended gracefully, her heart bursting with gratitude. She had become something wonderful—a girl who could fly free.
