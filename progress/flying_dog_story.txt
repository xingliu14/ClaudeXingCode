Max had always dreamed of flying. One morning, mysterious wings sprouted from his golden retriever body. At first, he panicked, but soon discovered freedom soaring through clouds. Children pointed and gasped as he glided above parks, his fur gleaming in sunlight. Other dogs watched in envy. Max learned to dive and loop, painting joy across the sky. He discovered being different wasn't a curse—it was a gift. Every evening, he'd rest on rooftops, reflecting on his life. Max wasn't just a dog anymore. He was a legend, a flying marvel inspiring wonder in everyone who witnessed his graceful flight.
